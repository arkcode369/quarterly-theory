# 33 - Advanced 4 Stage

## Konsep yang Diajarkan
1. Advanced 4 stage price delivery
2. Cara menggunakan 4 stage di setiap cycle

## Rangkuman

### Advanced 4 Stage Framework
Pendalaman dari **4 tahap price delivery** dengan level detail lebih tinggi.

**4 Stages:**
1. **Consolidation** (Stage 1) — Range-bound, accumulation
2. **Expansion** (Stage 2) — Breakout dari consolidation
3. **Retracement** (Stage 3) — Pullback setelah expansion
4. **Reversal/Distribution** (Stage 4) — Arah berbalik atau distribusi

### Penerapan di Setiap Cycle
- Setiap cycle (Yearly, Monthly, Weekly, Daily, 90min, Micro) memiliki **4 stage sendiri**
- HTF 4 stage mengandung LTF 4 stages di dalamnya
- Kunci: identifikasi **stage mana** di HTF, lalu gunakan LTF untuk entry

### Alignment 4 Stage
- HTF Stage 2 + LTF Stage 2 → **high probability continuation**
- HTF Stage 3 + LTF Stage 4 → **expect reversal**

### Integrasi dengan SSMT
- SSMT biasanya terjadi di **transisi antara stages** (terutama Stage 3 ke Stage 4)
- Confirmed SSMT di stage transition = highest probability setup

### How to Trade Each Stage
| Stage | Action |
|-------|--------|
| 1 (Consolidation) | Jangan trade, tunggu breakout |
| 2 (Expansion) | Trade with trend, entry pada retracement kecil |
| 3 (Retracement) | Cari SSMT untuk reversal entry |
| 4 (Reversal) | Entry counter-trend jika SSMT confirmed |

# 08 - Introduction to Sequential SMT (GAME CHANGER)

## Konsep yang Diajarkan
1. Sequential SMT

## Rangkuman

### Apa itu Sequential SMT?
SMT yang terjadi antara dua correlated markets selama **Q2, Q3, dan Q4**.

### Kegunaan Sequential SMT
1. Trade Q4
2. Prediksi weekly high/low

### Perbedaan Sequential SMT vs SMT Divergence

| | SMT Divergence | Sequential SMT |
|---|---------------|---------------|
| Referensi | True cycle opening | True afternoon opening |
| Timing | Q2 dan Q3 | Q3 dan Q4 |

### True Afternoon Opening
- Terjadi pada **12:00 PM**
- Berfungsi sama dengan true cycle opening (bearish = entry di atas, bullish = entry di bawah)

### Requirements untuk Sequential SMT
1. True afternoon opening
2. Hanya correlated markets (contoh: EURUSD + GBPUSD)
3. Premium dan discount
4. Timeframe yang benar
5. Target jelas
6. Entry point jelas

### Setup Premium & Discount

| Trend | Low | High |
|-------|-----|------|
| Bearish | Lowest swing Q3 (90min) | Highest swing Q4 (90min) |
| Bullish | Lowest swing Q4 (90min) | Highest swing Q3 (90min) |

### Timeframe
- Hanya 2 timeframe: **HTF (Daily)** dan **LTF (90 minute)**

### Targeting

| Trend | Target |
|-------|--------|
| Bullish | Q2/Q3 (Daily Q3) consolidation, equal swing highs |
| Bearish | Q2/Q3 (Daily Q3) consolidation, equal swing lows |

### Entry (Contoh Bullish)
1. Tunggu Q3 (Daily cycle) ter-expand selama Q4
2. Tunggu MSS selama Q4
3. Entry pada FVG

### Chart Setup
- HTF: Daily cycle — Q3 dan Q4
- LTF: 90-minute cycle — Q2, Q3, Q4 (di dalam Daily Q3/Q4)
